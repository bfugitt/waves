Waves carry Energy

Sound waves don't travel by carrying air molecules directly to your ears. They work by having air molecules bump back and forth, passing energy forward!

Wavelength & Pitch

When waves are packed closely together (short wavelength), they wiggle past your ears incredibly fast. Your brain hears this high frequency as a squeak!

Communication Tool

Humans, dolphins, and forest birds change wave frequencies and amplitude sizes to send specific safety warnings, greetings, and secrets.
